---
title: Editions
---

Application editions can scale up and scale out. You can scale up- and down in the same series, but once you scale up (example basic > standard), you cannot scale down again.

## Basic series
Basic series provide a read-only 5 GB pool of storage per app.

| Edition | vCPU | Memory | Scale | Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| B1 | 0.5 | 128 MB | 1/1 | - |
| B1 | 0.5 | 256 MB | 1/1 | - |

## Standard series
Standard series provide a read-only 50 GB pool of storage per app.

| Edition | vCPU | Memory | Scale | Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| S1 | 1 | 1 GB | 1/5 | - |
| S2 | 2 | 2 GB | 1/10 | - |
| S3 | 2 | 4 GB | 1/10 | - |

## Premium series
Premium series provide a read-only 250 GB pool of storage per app.

| Edition | vCPU | Memory | Scale | Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| P1 | 4 | 8 GB | 1/20 | - |
| P2 | 4 | 16 GB | 1/30 | - |
